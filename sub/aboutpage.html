About us:

A Home For Your Pet
is a welfare non-profit organisation based in the Northern Cape, Kimberley.
Animals are neglected, mistreated, abused and killed inhumanely and we aim
to imporove this by providing care, support and education to the animals. This
is a shelter that resuces abused and abandoned animals and they are so deserving
of love. We also sterilize and treat animals and do what we can to help the animals
living in poor environments.

Our Misson:

To advocate the safety and well-being of animals. To find good and
loving homes for all our adorable furry friends! Open your
hearts and homes for any one of our adordable friends

Our Vision:

Our vision is for all animals to live a life free of cruelty and suffering.
